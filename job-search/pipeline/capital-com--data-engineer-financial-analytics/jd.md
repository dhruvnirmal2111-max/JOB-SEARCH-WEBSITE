# Job Description — Data Engineer (Financial Analytics)
**Company:** Capital.com  
**Location:** Melbourne, Australia (Onsite)

---

## About Capital.com

Capital.com is a leading global fintech and CFD/trading platform with users across multiple jurisdictions worldwide. The company is ambitiously expanding its technology team and operates a multi-jurisdiction financial data platform backed by PostgreSQL and Apache Airflow.

---

## Role Overview

We're looking for a Data Engineer to join the Financial Analytics team. You'll be responsible for developing and maintaining data pipelines, transformation logic, and data quality checks in a complex, multi-jurisdiction financial data platform (PostgreSQL DWH + Airflow orchestration).

This role requires a high degree of autonomy — you'll reverse-engineer undocumented systems, identify root causes of data issues, and take end-to-end ownership of pipelines and financial calculation logic.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Implement enhancements and changes to existing reporting processes to improve accuracy, performance, and usability.
- Design and develop new reporting pipelines and datasets aligned with business requirements.
- Collaborate with business stakeholders to gather reporting requirements, clarify logic, and ensure outputs meet expectations.
- Build and maintain data quality checks and data validation frameworks within the financial data platform.
- Develop and optimise complex financial calculation logic in SQL (stored procedures, functions).
- Manage and maintain Apache Airflow DAGs: authoring, scheduling, dependency management, and failure handling.
- Take end-to-end ownership of pipelines — from ingestion through transformation to mart layer delivery.
- Identify and resolve root causes of data quality issues in a multi-jurisdiction financial data environment.

---

## Requirements

- **4+ years** in analytics engineering, data engineering, or similar data-focused roles.
- **Advanced PostgreSQL skills:** stored procedures and functions, complex CTEs, window functions, SCD2 patterns, query plan analysis and optimisation.
- **Strong understanding of data warehouse architecture:** staging, core, and data mart layers; incremental load patterns; slowly changing dimensions (SCD1/SCD2).
- **Hands-on Apache Airflow experience:** DAG authoring, scheduling, dependency management, and failure handling.
- **Practical experience implementing complex financial calculation logic in SQL.**
- High degree of autonomy: able to reverse-engineer undocumented systems, identify root causes, and take end-to-end ownership.
- Strong collaboration skills — ability to gather requirements from non-technical stakeholders.

## Nice to Have

- Experience with fintech/CFD/trading data platforms.
- Knowledge of multi-currency or multi-jurisdiction regulatory reporting.
- dbt or similar transformation framework experience.
- Python scripting for data pipeline automation.
