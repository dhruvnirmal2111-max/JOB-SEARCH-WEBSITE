# AGL — Data Scientist (Data Science Chapter)

> Source: owner-provided posting. Captured 2026-06-26. Teams: Acquisition Performance & Enablement; Product & Pricing Analytics.

## About the role
Two Data Scientists to join AGL's Data Science Chapter, partnering with the Acquisition Performance & Enablement and Product & Pricing Analytics teams. Leverage advanced analytics, machine learning and predictive modelling to solve complex business challenges, uncover growth opportunities and deliver data-driven insights that influence commercial decision-making. Cross-functional, collaborating with business stakeholders while connected to a community of data science specialists focused on innovation, knowledge sharing and best practice.

## What you'll be doing
- Develop advanced machine learning, predictive and optimisation models that drive customer growth, retention and commercial performance.
- Transform large, complex datasets into actionable insights that inform strategic decisions and create measurable business value.
- Partner with stakeholders across Product, Commercial, Technology and Analytics teams to solve high-impact business challenges.
- Design experiments, investigate customer behaviours and uncover opportunities to enhance products, pricing strategies and customer experiences.
- Bring insights to life through compelling data storytelling, visualisations and dashboards while championing innovation and best-practice data science.

## About you
- Proven experience developing predictive models, machine learning solutions and advanced analytics to solve complex business problems.
- Strong programming skills in Python, SQL, with the ability to work across large and complex datasets.
- Expertise in translating data into actionable insights and communicating recommendations to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Experience with data visualisation and reporting tools such as Power BI or Tableau, bringing insights to life through compelling storytelling.
- A curious and commercially minded approach, with a passion for innovation, experimentation and using data to drive customer and business outcomes.
