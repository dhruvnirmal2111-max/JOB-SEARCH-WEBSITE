Evaluates business requirements and performs basic data integration and analysis. Develops routine analytical and reporting tools utilizing integrated data. Provides technical support.

The Department of Neurology wants a data analyst for projects related to real-world evidence for the effectiveness, safety, and tolerability of diagnostics and treatments related to seizures, as well as the analysis of clinical trials for seizures.
