# Commercial & Procurement Analyst — BOUNCE

**Source:** JD pasted by owner (2026-07-22)
**Company:** BOUNCE (trampoline / active-entertainment network; Food & Beverage across venues)
**Team:** Finance, partnering with Operations, Food & Beverage, suppliers and senior stakeholders
**Reports to / works alongside:** Commercial Manager

## Overview
As Commercial & Procurement Analyst, you'll help drive smarter commercial decisions across one of the most dynamic parts of the BOUNCE business.

Sitting within our Finance team and partnering closely with Operations, Food & Beverage, suppliers and senior stakeholders, you'll combine analytics, commercial thinking and relationship building to identify opportunities that improve profitability, optimise spend and support business growth.

This is more than reporting and spreadsheets. You'll turn data into action, uncover opportunities to improve margins, support supplier negotiations, influence pricing decisions and help shape the future of our Food & Beverage offering across the BOUNCE network.

Working alongside the Commercial Manager, you'll play a key role in building stronger commercial capability, improving procurement outcomes and ensuring leaders have the insights they need to make confident decisions.

## What You'll Be Doing
- Analysing sales, costs, margins and supplier performance to identify opportunities for improvement
- Creating commercial insights and business cases that support pricing, procurement and operational decisions
- Supporting supplier negotiations through data-driven commercial analysis
- Assisting with end-to-end tender processes and supplier evaluations
- Managing contract milestones, renewals, pricing changes and compliance requirements
- Supporting inventory forecasting and stock planning decisions
- Developing reporting, dashboards and analytical tools to improve visibility and decision-making
- Identifying process improvements that enhance efficiency, reduce costs and strengthen procurement outcomes
- Building strong relationships with internal stakeholders and external supplier partners

## What You Bring to the Tribe
Naturally curious and commercially minded; enjoys digging into data, finding patterns and uncovering opportunities others might miss. Moves comfortably between analysis and action, translating numbers into practical recommendations. Confident building relationships, asking questions, working collaboratively across the business. Loves learning; thrives in a fast-paced environment.

## Key Skills & Attributes (how you work)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving mindset
- Naturally curious; translates data into meaningful commercial insights
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
- High attention to detail and strong organisational capability
- Proactive, adaptable, comfortable managing competing priorities
- Collaborative, willing to roll up sleeves
- Alignment with BOUNCE values: Passion, Unity, Accountability, Keeping it Fresh

## Technical & Professional Competencies (what you know & can do)
- 2–4 years' experience in finance, commercial analysis, procurement, business analysis or a similar role
- Advanced Excel skills; ability to analyse and interpret large datasets
- Understanding of commercial drivers including sales, costs, margins and profitability
- Experience with reporting and business intelligence tools highly regarded
- Experience supporting procurement, supplier management or commercial decision-making advantageous but not essential
