## Job Overview

Seeking a detail-oriented Data Analyst with strong Tableau expertise to transform complex data into actionable business insights. Works closely with stakeholders, develops dashboards, analyzes trends and supports data-driven decision-making across the organization.

## Key Responsibilities
- Gather, clean and analyze large datasets from multiple sources.
- Design and maintain interactive Tableau dashboards and reports.
- Create KPIs, scorecards and data visualizations for business stakeholders.
- Write SQL queries to extract and manipulate data.
- Identify trends, patterns and business opportunities through analysis.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand reporting requirements.
- Ensure data accuracy, integrity and quality.
- Present findings and recommendations to management.

## Who Can Apply
- Tableau Desktop and Tableau Server expertise.
- Strong SQL skills.
- Advanced Microsoft Excel.
- Data visualization and storytelling.
- Statistical analysis and reporting.
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
- Bachelor's degree in Data Analytics, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science or related field.
- 1 to 3 years of experience in Data Analytics or Business Intelligence.

Highly suitable for professionals transitioning from Reporting Analyst, MIS Executive, Business Analyst or Power BI Analyst positions.
