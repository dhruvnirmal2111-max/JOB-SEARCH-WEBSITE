# Melbourne Water — Data Analyst

**Source:** Position Description PDF (Melbourne Water, Doc ID 69425413, v2, reviewed Jun 2026)
**Job level:** EA Level 7
**Reports to:** Manager, Commercial & Analytics · 0 direct reports
**Location:** Docklands or Brooklyn (some travel to MW sites — ETP, WTP, Winneke, SERO, Docklands)

## Purpose
- Support the Works Delivery team — deliver efficient/effective maintenance regimes through analysis of maintenance data and processes.
- Lead data management and the delivery of maintenance and operational performance metrics to inform decision-making and drive improvement, while keeping data integrity.
- Provide Works Delivery reports and improve the quality of data captured in maintaining/operating assets.

## Key accountabilities
- Improve maintenance delivery + decision-making via effective data analysis and performance-measurement reporting.
- Identify, analyse and interpret trends and **use AI** to drive business-improvement opportunities, collaborating with Works Delivery teams toward customer, regulatory and financial objectives.
- Lead development/analysis of performance metrics across various databases, ensuring consistency and standardisation.
- Collaborate with maintenance service providers to improve data capture and quality.
- Actively use AI to streamline existing processes.
- Compliance with MW systems/policies (OH&S, Environment, Product Quality), IMS, safety & wellbeing.

## Key responsibilities
**Data Analytics**
- Lead/engage with Maintenance, Operations, Minor Capital and Maintenance Service Provider(s) on data analysis.
- Analytics across: maintenance performance, reliability, assets, operations, service providers.
- Timely data-based reports + dashboards; analysis adding significant value to maintenance regime reviews.
- Identify trends/patterns to inform improvement and corrective activities.
- Contribute to a data analytics framework; develop key indicators for benchmarking programs.
- Develop tools and **use AI** to help teams review/analyse program data.

**Drive Exceptional Performance**
- Improve data management processes; implement and monitor a QA program for data.
- Identify high-priority data issues; develop/implement controls to clean data.
- Document/review data processes for quality; build dashboards for maintenance & reliability.
- Identify improvement opportunities incl. AI; ensure data is high-quality and report-ready (external + internal KPI).
- Develop and generate monthly exception reports.

**Reporting**
- Participate in performance, condition, governance and external reporting.
- Drive the reporting framework, incorporating different systems into a reporting/analysis environment.
- Develop and manage dashboards (e.g. **Snowflake, Power BI, Power Automate, SQL Server**) presenting data from multiple datasets.
- Monthly analysis of maintenance, capex, incentives across Infrastructure Operations; benchmarking categories.

## Skills, knowledge & experience required
- Data analytics for maintenance of large/complex mechanical & electrical plant and equipment.
- **AI, data analytics, statistical methods, predictive modelling, querying relational databases, statistical programming** for large datasets.
- Measure/interpret/present data visually to enhance operational decision-making.
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
- High level of customer service; constructive team contributor.
- Influential in workgroups in a complex environment; strong communication and negotiation.
- Excellent organisational skills, balancing priorities.
- Experience with **Maximo**, MS Office and other IT asset-based systems.
- Experience with **Business Intelligence tools, Snowflake, SQL Server, Power BI and Power Automate**.
- Advanced spreadsheeting, flow-charting and statistical software.

## Other requirements
- Trade or tertiary qualification with significant industry experience.
- National Police Check · Medical Assessment · Victorian Driver's Licence.
- Industrial experience within maintenance systems and processes.

## Key relationships
- **Internal:** Works Delivery, Asset Management Services, Waterways & Catchment Operations, Service Delivery Leadership, other groups.
- **External:** third-party delivery partners (esp. maintenance Service Providers), specialist consultants.
