## Core mission
Be the analytical engine behind HTB's most important client relationships. Use data to prove the ROI of the cybersecurity training platform: navigate complex datasets with SQL to uncover how training impacts human risk, then use storytelling to present insights to executive-level (USG / Federal agency) stakeholders.

## Tools & tech
- Looker (primary) and Tableau
- SQL on BigQuery (Snowflake a plus / legacy)
- Python for analysis, automation, statistics
- Excel for modeling and ad-hoc work
- Comfort with AI-assisted tooling (LLM copilots) to accelerate analysis and prototyping

## What you'll do
- **Strategic client consultation** — partner with Federal agency stakeholders; translate compliance/security needs into data-driven success plans; turn USG leadership business requirements into technical data solutions.
- **ROI modeling & data storytelling** — SQL + Python to extract/shape large datasets, surfacing correlations between training engagement and reduced security incidents. Build interactive Looker/Tableau dashboards and presentations that make ROI legible at every level of the USG.
- **Actionable behavioral insights** — deep-dive analysis on user behavior and simulation logs to identify high-risk cohorts; tailored recommendations to optimize training curriculum based on engagement signals (completion velocity, adoption, drop-off).
- **Technical data integrity** — audit the flow of training telemetry from platform to reporting layer; Python/SQL for rapid prototyping of risk models and ad-hoc "what-if" analysis for client presentations.
- **Stakeholder & relationship management** — part of Executive Business Reviews (EBRs); partner with Product and Data Engineering so platform telemetry keeps pace with reporting needs.
