# Puffy — Experimentation Analyst (Data Analyst, Experimentation / CRO)

_Source: pasted JD (LinkedIn/careers). Captured 2026-07-16. E-commerce / D2C (mattress & home), $1B+ platform, remote-friendly analytics team._

## The Opportunity

Own Puffy's experimentation program across a $1B+ e-commerce platform — from spotting where customers drop off, to proposing what to test next, to telling the business exactly what worked and why. Days are spent running A/B tests, analyzing conversion funnels, and writing your own SQL and Python to get answers. You pull data from Shopify, GA4, Google Ads, and BigQuery yourself. You design the test, run the statistical analysis, and deliver a recommendation clear enough that marketing, UX, and development can act on it the same week. General analysts and program managers without hands-on technical and experimentation skills won't be a fit.

## Trajectory

Growth path is fluid. From this seat you could evolve into:
- **Senior Experimentation Analyst** — owning high-stakes tests end to end.
- **Analytics Engineer** — building the data layer and self-serve tooling the whole team relies on.

"What takes 4-5 years elsewhere happens in 1-2 here."

## Who you'll work with

Sit within the Analytics team; collaborate across the org:
- **Senior Management** — recommendations shape site and campaign decisions
- **Digital Marketing** — tie ad platform data to on-site behavior across the full funnel
- **CRO** — partner on hypothesis prioritization and test design
- **Dev & QA** — translate winning tests into production and validate implementation
- **Finance** — connect experimentation outcomes to revenue impact
- **Customer Support** — surface post-purchase friction that feeds upstream test ideas

Team includes alumni from Apple, Deutsche Bank, Deloitte.

## What you'll own

- Analyze A/B tests end-to-end — what worked, what didn't, why — and deliver decision-ready recommendations, not metrics readouts.
- Map user journeys across devices and traffic sources; pinpoint friction, drop-offs, conversion leaks; convert them into prioritized, testable hypotheses.
- Dig into data at segment, device, source, and journey-step level to find non-obvious drivers top-line reporting misses.
- Build and maintain your own data pipelines across Shopify, GA4, Google Ads, and BigQuery — no waiting in a queue.
- Contribute to a structured hypothesis pipeline: prioritize by impact, write clear test briefs, define success criteria upfront, push proposals to execution.
- Orchestrate agentic AI workflows to accelerate analysis, using SQL and Python as the foundation to direct, validate, and scale analytical output.
- Collaborate with marketing, UX, and development to translate findings into site changes, campaign adjustments, and creative decisions.

## What you bring

- 3+ years in data analysis, experimentation, or CRO within e-commerce, D2C, or any high-traffic transactional website (fintech, marketplaces, SaaS, banking).
- Strong SQL — complex joins, window functions, CTEs across large datasets without relying on pre-built dashboards.
- Working Python (or R) for data manipulation, statistical analysis, and automation.
- Proven A/B testing — hypothesis design through statistical analysis to business recommendation; solid grasp of sample sizing, significance thresholds, common pitfalls.
- Hands-on GA4 — event-based tracking, custom explorations, tying GA4 data to other sources.
- Experience integrating ad platform data (Google Ads, Meta) with site analytics for the full acquisition-to-conversion funnel.
- Ability to independently connect to and pull from multiple data sources (APIs, warehouses, flat files) without engineering support.
- Sharp analytical communication — complex findings distilled into concise recommendations for non-technical stakeholders.
- Proactive and curious — spot testing opportunities and chase root causes before anyone asks.

## Good to have

- BigQuery or AWS data warehousing, including scheduled queries and data modeling.
- Session replay / heatmap tools (FullStory, Hotjar) for qualitative behavioral analysis.
- Looker, Tableau, or similar dashboarding tools.
