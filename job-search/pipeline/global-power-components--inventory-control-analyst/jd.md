# Global Power Components — Inventory Control Analyst

_Source: pasted JD (Wellfound). Captured 2026-07-16. GPC = privately held manufacturer of UL heavy-gauge steel fuel tanks, trailers, generator enclosures, integrated electrical distribution. Milwaukee HQ; **remote only, hires everywhere**. Full-time, ~2 yrs exp. No visa sponsorship._

## Position summary
As GPC expands and introduces new inventory parts (including manufacturing stock) into its system, they need an Inventory Control Analyst to **monitor inventory accuracy, identify discrepancies, and proactively address data gaps** to ensure strong system integrity and operational efficiency.

## Key responsibilities
**Inventory accuracy & analysis**
- Monitor and analyze inventory data to identify discrepancies, trends, and systemic issues.
- Investigate root causes of variances between physical inventory and ERP records.
- Develop and maintain reports, dashboards, and KPIs to track inventory performance.
- Perform reconciliation analysis and recommend corrective actions.

**Process improvement & controls**
- Evaluate inventory processes and identify gaps (esp. manufacturing-stock integration).
- Establish/refine inventory control procedures for accuracy and consistency.
- Partner cross-functionally to implement corrective + preventative actions.
- Drive standardization of inventory-related data and transaction processes.

**System & data integrity**
- Act as liaison between operations and ERP/system teams for inventory issues.
- Ensure proper use + accuracy of inventory transactions in ERP/MRP systems.
- Support system enhancements, testing, and process improvements.

**Audit & compliance**
- Support cycle-count programs by analyzing results, identifying recurring discrepancies.
- Assist internal audits; ensure compliance with inventory control standards.
- Provide insight/analysis on discrepancies (not routine counting).

## Qualifications
- **Minimum:** Bachelor's in Finance, Business, Supply Chain, or related; 2+ yrs in inventory analysis, supply chain analytics, or data analysis; strong analytical/problem-solving; **advanced Excel (pivot tables, lookups, data analysis)**.
- **Preferred:** ERP/MRP systems (Epicor, SAP, Oracle); analyzing inventory in manufacturing; data-viz tools (Power BI, Tableau); understanding of inventory control, costing, reconciliation.
