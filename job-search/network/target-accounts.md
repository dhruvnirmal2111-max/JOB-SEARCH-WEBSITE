# Target Accounts — Dream Companies (Track B: proactive)

> Companies worth building relationships at **regardless of whether they have an open role right now**. Track B feeds Track A: by the time one of these posts, you already know people inside, so you apply warm instead of cold.
>
> **Confirm-first rule (same as the scout):** research proposes → you set `Pursue? = yes` → only then does `/connect <company>` build outreach. Keep this to companies you'd genuinely take a role at (fits `profile/preferences.md`, salary floor ≥95k).

## Warmth scale
- **cold** — no contacts yet
- **warm** — 1+ connections accepted / light conversation underway
- **hot** — active conversation or a referral-ready contact inside

## Notes
- **"Open role now?"** is the Track B → Track A bridge. When it flips to *yes*, run `/quick-apply` or `/apply` and let outreach reference the relationship you've already built ("we've been chatting — I just applied to X").
- The Daily Scout can append candidate companies here (with `Pursue?` blank) the same way it appends postings to `targets/shortlist.md`.
