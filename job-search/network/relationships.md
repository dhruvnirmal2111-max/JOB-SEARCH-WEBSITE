# Relationship Pipeline (Track B CRM)

> Every person you're building a relationship with, across all target accounts — **decoupled from any single job**. This is the networking equivalent of `pipeline/pipeline.md`. The commander reads `Next touch` dates during `/standup`; you send every message manually (review gate).
>
> Stage flow: **identified → request-sent → connected → conversing → referral-ready → referred/intro'd** (or **dormant**).

## Stage definitions
- **identified** — found + verified they currently work there; no contact yet
- **request-sent** — connection request sent (≤300 chars, no ask)
- **connected** — accepted; relationship channel open
- **conversing** — at least one real back-and-forth
- **referral-ready** — warm enough to ask for a referral/intro when a role fits
- **referred / intro'd** — they've referred you or made an introduction
- **dormant** — no response / paused (don't keep pinging)

## Personas & tone (Track B = relationship, not a job ask)
- **Peer** → advice-seeking / shared-path curiosity
- **Senior** → respectful curiosity about their work
- **Manager** → genuine interest in the team + what they're building
- **Recruiter** → express interest in the company for *future* roles (keep-warm)

## Default nurture cadence (all drafts — you send manually)
1. **Day 0** — connection request (persona tone, ≤300 chars, **no ask**)
2. **Day 3–5 after accept** — value touch: comment on their work / share something useful / one thoughtful question. No ask.
3. **Day 10–14** — soft ask: 15-min advice chat, or a genuine question about their team/work.
4. **On a role posting at their company** — warm referral ask (Track A activates and references this relationship).

> Cadence is a default, not a script — skip/adjust per person. Never automate sending. Verify the person currently works at the company before reaching out.
