# 30-Day Job Hunt Playbook

Goal: **a signed offer within 30 days.** This is the campaign the `job-hunt-commander` paces against. Every artifact is a draft until you approve it (`/review-outreach`, `/review-calendar`).

## How to run it day to day

```
Morning   → /standup            (auto at 7:00 AEST once schedules are on)
During day→ do the top actions; /apply <company> for new targets
As needed → /review-outreach    (approve messages, then YOU send them)
            /review-calendar     (approve events → Google Calendar)
Sunday    → /weekly-review        (auto at 18:00 AEST)
```

Setup order (one time): `/intake` → `/find-targets` → `/activate-schedules`. Connect Google Calendar once (see `calendar-sync` skill).

## Targets / metrics (the scoreboard)

| Metric | 30-day target |
|--------|---------------|
| Applications submitted | 20–30 (≈1–2/day) |
| Outreach contacts engaged | 25+ (5 per active application) |
| First-round interviews | 4–6 |
| Onsite / final loops | 2–3 |
| Offers | 1+ |

If a weekly review shows you're behind the pace, the commander rebalances next week toward the lagging metric.

## Phase 1 — Days 1–3: Foundation
- **Day 1:** `/intake` — parse resume, lock preferences, set start date. Connect Google Calendar.
- **Day 2:** `/find-targets` — research + rank 12–20 roles; pick the ones to pursue (`Pursue? = yes`).
- **Day 3:** `/apply` your top 2 targets (full drafts). `/review-outreach` + `/review-calendar`. `/activate-schedules`.
- **Exit criteria:** preferences locked, ≥10-row shortlist, 2 applications drafted, schedules + calendar live.

## Phase 2 — Days 4–10: Launch volume
- `/apply` to 3–5 more targets across the week (aim ~1/day).
- Send week-1 outreach (recruiter + 1 peer per active target) after `/review-outreach`.
- Start the **#1 skill-gap learning block** from each `analysis.md` (calendar-blocked).
- Submit applications yourself; log them so status flips to **Applied**.
- **Exit criteria (Day 10):** 7–10 applications submitted, outreach started on each, learning underway.

## Phase 3 — Days 11–20: Scale + convert
- Keep applying to the best remaining targets; replenish the shortlist with `/find-targets` if thin.
- Week-2 outreach (hiring manager + 2nd peer); follow up once on non-responders after 5–7 business days (commander stages reminders).
- Interview prep from `analysis.md` for any role that responds; calendar-block prep before each interview.
- **Mid-point `/weekly-review` (≈Day 14):** are interviews materializing? If not, adjust targeting/outreach.
- **Exit criteria (Day 20):** 15–20 applications, several conversations in motion, ≥2 interviews booked.

## Phase 4 — Days 21–30: Close
- Focus shifts from volume to **conversion**: deep interview prep, thank-you notes within 24h, week-3 senior-leader outreach only where warranted.
- Prepare negotiation: comp research per offer-stage company, your floor (from `preferences.md`), and talking points.
- Keep a thin top-of-funnel going (don't stop applying until an offer is signed).
- **Exit criteria:** ≥1 offer in hand; negotiate; decide.

## Guardrails
- Never mark "Applied"/"Sent" unless you actually did it.
- Never let the system send a message or create a calendar event without your approval.
- Truthful resumes only.
